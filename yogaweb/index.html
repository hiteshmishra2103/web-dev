What is Diva Yoga?

A woman plays many different roles in a lifetime - mother, sister, daughter, friend, lover - although she essentially is always A Diva on the inside. A goddess blessed with the power of bringing forth life, a Goddess that completes the planet, a Goddess that balances energies with both her power and tenderness.Diva Yoga is a one-of-a-kind program committed to the transformation of women both physically and mentally . We believe that every woman is a Diva - and we wish to help her realise this truth. We aim to bring her innate diva-ness to the surface, where her inner beauty can shine throughDiva Yoga is a specially curated experience for the Diva we see in you. The mission is to bring to every woman, the delight and discovery of womanhood in totality.
